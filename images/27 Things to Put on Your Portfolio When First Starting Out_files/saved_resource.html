                                              Hey Brittany, Great question! It's usually best to only include the things that relate to the jobs you're looking for -- especially on your resume since it's one-page. A few Google searches should bring you information on how to spin past work experience to be relevant to the new roles you're looking off.

                                              Personally, I leave completely unrelated pieces of experience off my resume and LinkedIn. If you look at my LinkedIn you'll see that I include fun facts in my summary (https://www.linkedin.com/in.... That's how I incorporate the unrelated things ;)
